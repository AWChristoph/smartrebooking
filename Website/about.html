Why we spent our time on SmartRebooking

Our team consists of these people who live their life. This means we're planning trips as everybody else, but during the journey we're changing them 'cause we want to do something different. Most of the time these kind of changes are expensive and take a lot time manage.
That's why we developed SmartRebooking, 'cause canceling and rebooking flight tickets are one of the most annoying and expensive changes of a journey.
It is our goal to make trips fun and flexible again, just as your life requires it!
